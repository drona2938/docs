<Warning>
  Complying with Meta's 24-Hour Rule for Drip Campaigns



  Meta enforces a 24-hour rule for promotional messaging on Messenger. You can only send promotional messages to users who have interacted with your business within the past 24 hours. Interactions include subscribing to your bot, clicking a button, or starting a conversation.



  Failure to comply may result in restrictions on your ability to send messages through Messenger.
</Warning>
